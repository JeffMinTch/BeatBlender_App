Licensing

We make music licensing as simple as streaming music online, and guide you through each step of clearing your samples.

License To Release

If you want to release your new song, you must first get a sample license. We have three price categories, and you know what the fee will be even before you download the audio track. The category is listed below each track when you dig. No surprises.
