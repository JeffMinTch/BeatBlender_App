The BeatBlender Open Source Project was founded 2020 by artists, sound enginneers and copyright lawyers that are convinced that the current copyright system destroys a creative, diverse and Free Music Culture environment in terms of sampling.

We have created a sample licensing system that on the one hand allows artists to sample high quality music commercially without additional costs, and on the other hand compensates the creators of the embedded samples appropriately.

To clear sample rights artists usually have to buy it from the copyright owner or use licenses under creative commons .
